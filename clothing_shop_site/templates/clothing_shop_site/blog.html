    How to style a Quiff

    The quiff is a classic hairstyle characterized by volume, height, and texture in the front section of the hair. To style a quiff, start with clean, towel-dried hair and apply a volumizing product to the roots. Then, blow-dry the hair while lifting the roots to create volume. Next, section off the front part of the hair and gently tease it at the roots for added height. Shape the hair into a quiff by pushing it forward and upward, experimenting with different heights and angles. Secure the quiff with a strong-hold hairspray or styling product. For finishing touches, use your fingers to add texture and definition, if desired. Remember to adapt the steps based on your hair type and preferences.
